Hotels Recommendations

Here are some good hotels that French Fries organized for you.

Hotel Duquesne Eiffel

Paris, 3.2 km from the city centre. In the shadow of the Invalides, 5 minutes walk from the famous Champs de Mars and the Eiffel Tower, the Hotel Duquesne Eiffel offers welcoming and comfortable rooms.

Price: NT $5909

Hotel Austin's Saint Lazare

This Austin's hotel is located in central Paris, next to Saint-Lazare Train Station and 801 m from Opéra Garnier. It offers soundproofed guest rooms with air-conditioning, flat-screen satellite TV and free WiFi.

Price: NT $4220

Hotel Longchamp Elysées

This designer hotel is ideally located in Paris, 293 m from Trocadero, the Champ Elysees and the Eiffel Tower is nearby. Most rooms open out onto a small garden.

Price: NT $6630

Restaurants Recommendations

French Fries discovers the real culinary treats of Paris for you.

Le Chalet Savoyard

Right on Rue de Charonne, Le Chalet Savoyard is a simple restaurant that doesn’t fuss too much about décor but gets straight to the point: Savoyard specialties. p>

Prices: Low / Medium

Les Résistants

Welcome to a chic country setting in the heart of Château d’Eau, just steps away from Place de la République.

Prices: Medium

Brasserie Auteuil

A great terrace in the summer with a modern restaurant inside, a hopping all-season rooftop terrace, and a ski chalet in winter, the Brasserie Auteuil is a wonderful place for food and drinks any time of year.

Prices: Medium / High
